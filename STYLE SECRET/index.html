Popular Collection

Our Designers

Masaba Gupta

She is deserving of her position as one of the top 10 fashion designers in India. Her birth year was 1989, and at the age of 19, she presented her first collection at the Lakme Fashion Week in Mumbai. She is one of those young fashion designers that aren't afraid to try new things and is always coming up with innovative concepts. Masaba is well-known for coming up with one-of-a-kind designs and prints that no other designer has ever tried to replicate.

Manish Malhotra

The name Manish Malhotra has become almost synonymous with fashion in India. The desire of every Indian bride is to wear a lehenga designed by Manish Malhotra. After breaking into the Indian fashion industry for the first time in 1998, he began his career as a costume designer and eventually made his way up to become the most well-known designer.

Sabyasachi Mukherjee

This is a name that does not need to be introduced, particularly after he created the wedding lehengas that were worn by the brides of Bollywood. Sabyasachi was the designer of choice for a large number of brides in Bollywood, including Deepika Padukone, Anushka Sharma, Priyanka Chopra, Bipasha Basu, and Soha Ali Khan, amongst many more.

Tarun Tahiliani

The name Tarun Tahiliani is an example of fashion fusion. He is well-known for the styles he creates, which are distinguished by their combination of European and Indian inspirations. He is one of the first fashion designers to come out of India who has established a reputation for himself in the world of international fashion. The ensemble was India's first multi-designer boutique when it opened in 1987, and he and his wife, Sailaja "Sal" Tahiliani, were the co-founders of the business.

Anita Dongre

House of Anita Dongre, as well as its other sub-brands, including AND and Anita Dongre Grassroot, are all her creations. As a woman who has fulfilled her aspirations, she is an inspiration. She is quite skilled at incorporating timeless details into up-to-the-minute outfits.
